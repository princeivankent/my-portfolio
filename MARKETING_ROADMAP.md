## Service Offerings

### 1. E-Commerce Platform Development
**Ideal For:** Retail businesses, wholesalers, B2B distributors wanting to establish/expand online presence

**Scope:**
- Custom branded e-commerce storefront
- Product catalog management with variants (sizes, colors, etc.)
- Shopping cart with abandoned cart recovery
- Payment gateway integration (Stripe, PayPal, local options)
- Inventory synchronization with backend
- Order management & fulfillment tracking
- Customer accounts with purchase history
- Analytics & sales reporting

**Technology Stack:**
- **Frontend:** Angular (responsive, modern UX)
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express OR .NET Core (scalable, high-performance)
- **Database:** PostgreSQL/MongoDB (depending on needs)
- **Hosting:** AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud (scalable infrastructure)

**Pricing Strategy:**
- **Project-based:** $8,000 - $25,000 depending on complexity
- **Ongoing support/maintenance:** $500-$2,000/month
- **Per-transaction fees:** 1-2% on transactions (optional, recurring revenue model)

**Timeline:** 6-12 weeks for MVP, 3-4 months for full-featured platform

**ROI for Client:**
- Typically achieve 20-40% of retail sales via e-commerce within 12 months
- Reduced operational overhead vs. brick-and-mortar

---

### 2. POS (Point of Sale) System
**Ideal For:** Retail stores, restaurants, cafes, physical retail locations wanting integrated operations

**Scope:**
- Real-time sales tracking at checkout
- Multi-location support (chain stores)
- Employee/cashier management & permissions
- Barcode scanning & product lookup
- Customer loyalty program integration
- Receipt generation (thermal printer support)
- Daily/weekly/monthly reports & analytics
- Inventory auto-deduction with low-stock alerts
- Payment processing (card readers, cash, mobile payments)
- Offline capability (works without internet temporarily)

**Technology Stack:**
- **Frontend:** Angular (desktop & tablet-optimized UI)
- **Backend:** .NET Core (fast, reliable, enterprise-grade)
- **Database:** SQL Server/PostgreSQL (ACID compliance for financial transactions)
- **Hardware:** Integrations with POS hardware (barcode scanners, card readers, printers)

**Pricing Strategy:**
- **One-time setup:** $5,000 - $15,000
- **Monthly subscription:** $299-$999/month (scales with features)
- **Per-location fee:** +$199/month per additional location
- **Payment processing revenue share:** 1.5-2.5% per transaction

**Timeline:** 8-14 weeks for implementation + hardware setup

**ROI for Client:**
- Reduced transaction times (30% faster checkouts)
- Better inventory visibility (prevents stockouts/overstock)
- Labor efficiency (fewer manual processes)
- Data-driven decision making (detailed analytics)

---

### 3. Inventory Management System
**Ideal For:** E-commerce, retail, wholesale, 3PL companies managing multiple warehouses

**Scope:**
- Real-time inventory tracking across multiple locations
- SKU/barcode management with variants
- Automated reorder points & purchase order generation
- Warehouse management (bin locations, picking/packing workflows)
- Supplier integration & order tracking
- Forecast analytics (demand planning)
- Reporting dashboards (turnover, aging stock, profitability by SKU)
- API for third-party integrations (e-commerce, ERP, shipping)
- Mobile app for warehouse operations (scanning, picking)

**Technology Stack:**
- **Frontend:** Angular (web) + React Native (mobile)
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express OR .NET Core
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (robust for complex queries)
- **Real-time:** WebSocket integration for live inventory updates

**Pricing Strategy:**
- **One-time setup:** $10,000 - $30,000
- **Monthly subscription:** $800-$2,500/month (scales with SKU count)
- **Per-API-call:** Optional usage-based pricing
- **Premium features:** Advanced forecasting, AI-based recommendations (+$500/month)

**Timeline:** 10-16 weeks for full deployment

**ROI for Client:**
- 25-35% reduction in carrying costs (better stock management)
- 15-25% reduction in stockouts
- $2-5 per SKU per month operational savings
